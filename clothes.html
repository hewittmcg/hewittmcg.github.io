The Clothes I Wear the Most

March 2026

I have what I'd describe as a "moderate" number of clothes, but it seems like I gravitate towards wearing a small subset of them the majority of the time. I thought it'd be useful (for me) to go through and document what I end up wearing the most. I tend to be much less picky when buying things then when wearing them, so I have a ton of clothes that never get worn where I probably could've known this when buying them by being a bit more honest with myself.

It does seem like the quality of clothing matters a lot here; regardless of cost, the clothing I wear the most tends to be cut of heavier fabric. I also have a bunch of clothes that I bought that are really loud and I can't really wear that often. I should probably avoid this in future.

The Flat Head FN-D111

These are awesome -- they're the pair of pants I wear the most. They go pretty well with most things, and have broken in really nicely and are quite soft now. I did have to get a pocket and a few holes repaired recently after ~1.5 years of wearing them every 2-3 days.

Gap Denim Jacket

I have no idea what this jacket is actually called; I bought it on clearance and I couldn't find it online. The fabric is this weird denim/polyester blend. I love how it looks and fits, but it's starting to feel kind of flimsy with age, and isn't aging very well.

House of Blanks 1009 Heavyweight T-Shirt

I really like House of Blanks. They make relatively affordable basics that are made in Canada and feel premium. I wear this shirt so much that I had to fish it out of the laundry to take this photo, hence the wrinkles.

House of Blanks 3008 Pullover Hooded Sweatshirt

I wear this hoodie a ton. It's very well-made and is comfortable but also looks decent. I bought a quarter-zip and a crewneck from House of Blanks because of how happy I am with this, but I was a bit unimpressed. I think their baggy/boxy fits work well when broken up by the hoodie pocket, but without that the fit is too loose for my liking.
