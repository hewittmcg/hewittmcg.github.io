The Clothes I Wear the Most

March 2026

I have what I'd describe as a "moderate" number of clothes, but I gravitate toward wearing a small subset of them most of the time. I thought it'd be useful to go through and document what I end up reaching for. I tend to be much less picky when buying things than when wearing them; as such, I have a ton of clothes that never get worn, which I probably could've predicted if I'd been more honest with myself at the time of purchase.

Quality seems to matter a lot here; regardless of cost, the clothes I wear most tend to be cut from heavier fabric. I also have a bunch of loud pieces that I can't wear that often. I should probably avoid this in future.

I'm writing this mainly to make myself think about my clothes, but hopefully it's useful to anyone who stumbles on it.

The Flat Head FN-D111

These are awesome — they're the pair of pants I wear the most. They go pretty well with most things, and have broken in really nicely and are quite soft now. I did have to get a pocket and a few holes repaired recently after ~1.5 years of wearing them every 2-3 days.

Gap Denim Jacket

I have no idea what this jacket is called; I bought it on clearance and I couldn't find it online. The fabric is this weird denim/polyester blend. I love how it looks and fits, but it's starting to feel flimsy.

House of Blanks 1009 Heavyweight T-Shirt

I really like House of Blanks. They make relatively affordable basics that are made in Canada and feel premium. I wear this shirt so much that I had to fish it out of the laundry to take this photo, hence the wrinkles.

House of Blanks 3008 Pullover Hooded Sweatshirt

I wear this hoodie a ton. It's very well-made and is comfortable but also looks decent. I bought a quarter-zip and a crewneck from House of Blanks too, but was a bit unimpressed. I think their baggy/boxy fits work well when broken up by the hoodie pocket, but without that the fit is too loose for my liking.
